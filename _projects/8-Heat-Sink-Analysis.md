This project is for MAE 3240, Heat Transfer.  It involves analyzing heat transfer, efficiency, and effectiveness across a cylindrical pin fin, and then applying this fin to a 5x5 fin array, making a heat sink.  The efficiency and thermal resistance of the heat sink is found and used to analyze the effect of using a heat sink to disperse heat from a thin film heater.
